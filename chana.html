Gram Farming and Agriculture

• Growing Conditions:

Chickpeas are a warm-season crop that grows well in temperate climates. They thrive in well-drained soils with a pH level between 6 and 7.

• Watering Needs:

Chickpeas are drought-tolerant and require relatively low amounts of water compared to other crops.

• Planting and Harvesting:

Chickpeas are typically sown in early spring and harvested in late summer or early fall.

• Pests and Diseases:

Chickpeas are generally resistant to pests but can be affected by fungal diseases like ascochyta blight, which can be managed with proper crop rotation and disease-resistant varieties. Gram Farming and Agriculture
